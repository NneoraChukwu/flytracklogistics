Who We Are

Built for Nigeria's Toughest Roads

FlyTrack Logistics is a leading road-freight company specialising in efficient, secure, and timely transportation of goods across Nigeria and into neighbouring Cameroon. Established in 2018, we have grown rapidly to become a trusted partner for businesses requiring reliable haulage services.

Our extensive network of 14 depots enables coverage of key commercial routes in northern Nigeria and cross-border operations. We focus on bulk cargo, industrial goods, agricultural produce, and general freight — delivering with precision in challenging road conditions.

What We Offer

Our Freight Services

Comprehensive road freight solutions tailored to meet diverse client needs across Nigeria and Cameroon.

Full Truck Load (FTL)

Dedicated trucks for large-volume shipments — direct, point-to-point delivery with full cargo control.

Less-than-Truck Load (LTL)

Consolidated shipping for smaller consignments, optimising costs without sacrificing reliability.

Bulk & Specialised Haulage

Agricultural produce, industrial materials, construction supplies, and temperature-sensitive goods.

Cross-Border Freight

Seamless Nigeria–Cameroon cargo movement with expert customs documentation and border handling.

Contract Logistics

Long-term dedicated fleet services for high-volume clients needing consistent, predictable coverage.

Warehousing & Distribution

Short-term storage and last-mile distribution from our depot locations across the north.

Cargo Tracking & Reporting

Real-time updates via phone/WhatsApp, with GPS-enabled digital visibility platform in progress.
